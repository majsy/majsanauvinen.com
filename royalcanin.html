royal family by royal canin

development

As part of my internship as a front-end developer at MediaMonks I was a part of building the Royal Family by Royal Canin. It's a platform that lets the user explore different dog breeds, find it's perfect dog based on preferences, living situation and needs as well as create a personal account to get tailored updates.

Concept and design by DDB & Tribal Amsterdam and production by MediaMonks, where I did templating for the site which included HTML, CSS (Sass) and Javascript (Typescript).
