concept/strategy

During a three day cross program workshop at Hyper Island we had WWF as a client. The brief was to come up with a new engaging fundraising solution that would increase loyalty and the amount of donations to support the endangered wildlife. Our insights were that people want to feel like they're making active choices when donating, rather than feeling guilt. And also that people do spend a lot of time making active choices (with no external forces) when shopping online.

We created a concept for a new way of donating in an e-commerce environment. It's a map that showcases all of WWFs on-going projects which can be filtered according to region, animals, environment and current priority level. When you've found the specific project(s) you want to support you enter the value of your donation and add it to the cart. You can keep track of your friends' donations as well as see how your own donations make a difference by following the changing status of the causes.
