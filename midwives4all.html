midwives4all

concept/strategy/development/copy

Together with three classmates (aka tiny studio) I created a campaign for Swedens's Ministry of Foreign Affairs' initiativ midwives4all. The brief was to raise the problems of maternal and newborn death in low income countries, but mainly focus on spreading awareness about the benefits of midwifery and the important role midwives have for ensuring a safe motherhood.

The long term goal is for maternal and newborn health to be a prioritzed topic in every country and government. To get people talking we used thunderclap, which is a powerful crowd speaking tool, to reach out to as many people as possible. In addition to that we created a twitter recruit where you target a country and shuffle an influencial twitter user whom to encourage to join the cause.

Check out the campaign: speakupformidwifery.com!
