2 cool 4 gluten

concept/art direction/development

A few months ago I got diagnosed with gluten intolerance and ever since I've been struggling to find nice meat- and gluten free recipes (which is not only salad!). Inspired by awesome "whatthefuckshouldimakefordinner", I created 2cool4gluten, a veggie gluten free recipe generator.
