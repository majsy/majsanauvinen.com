whatkindof.me

concept/art direction/development/copy

A project I did together with a classmate where the task simply was to build a website related to internet privacy that would contribute to the world in some way. Our insights were that people don't put so much value in their password security, but easily share their private information without even viewing the source.

We created a cute and innocent looking bait-and-switch quiz to remind people that the internet can be a dangerous place for those who're not cautious. Check out the full quiz: whatkindof.me
