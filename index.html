i’m majsan, a colorful source of energy, curious explorer and professional ice breaker. I believe in great communication as a tool for making the world a bit better, whether it’s about creating an awesome campaign for a good cause or just doing a funky dance.

For me it’s important to build a great group culture in able to work efficiently, and of course have fun at the same time. What really motivates me is to conquer new learnings, meet new inspiring people to gain perspective and also push my boundaries.

Creating solid concepts and coding sexy front-end is something that gets me going, and something I want to keep doing. Since I’m also born with two sharp eyes and a cocky left brain half I can’t help but make sure that all the pieces fall into place nicely, even from a distance.
