fastighetsbyrån

concept/strategy/copy

A two day brief challenge we had together with Sweden's biggest real estate agency, Fastighetsbyrån. The brief was to come up with an idea for how Fastighetsbyrån could display and spread their properties in an innovative and different way, and in return attract more visitors and potential buyers to the showings.

Our concept was based on the insight that during Stockholm Pride week couples are queuing for hours outside of Stockholm city hall just to get a drop-in marriage ceremony, which lasts only for a few minutes. Since Fastighetsbyrån has plenty of empty properties just waiting to get filled with love, our idea was for Fastighetsbyrån to invite these eager lovebirds to instead get married in their beautiful houses. Fastighetsbyrån would fix the marriage registrar and the couple invite their family and friends for a loving moment in the property. The ceremony will also be open for visitors who want to experience a showing filled with love rather than empty walls, making Fastighetsbyrån Sweden's most lovingful real estate agency.
