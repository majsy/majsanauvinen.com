affordable art fair

concept/art direction/strategy

Affordable Art Fair is a dynamic art fair which today is located in 14 different countries around the world. Their vision is to democratize the art scene by offering art to affordable prices which can be bought at the fairs. AAF wants to make art less pretentious and instead encourage the multitude to enjoy art by for example eduacating about different art expressions on their site. Our brief was basically to come up with a campaign idea for how to sell more early bird tickets and attract more people (and potential buyers) to the Stockholm fair.

Since AAF's core value is that 'art should be fun for everyone', we came up with a concept for an art tool for creating fun portraits inspired by different art expressions. The tool "Artify Me" gives the opportunity to explore and learn about different expressions, while at the same time make a nice looking portrait which will reward the user with a discounted entrence fee. All the submitted portraits will be showcased in the gallery where a weekly voting contest will be held and the winner will get free tickets to the fair. As an extension of the campaign, AAF will place a few photo booths in public places of Stockholm where you can get an analogue preview of Artify Me.
